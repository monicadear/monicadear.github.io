Monica "Nikki" Flores is a Certified ScrumMasterÂ® and Certified CPACC technical project manager at Lullabot, where she's managed a portfolio of $10mm in projects to develop enterprise web platforms for state governments, tech companies, non-governmental organizations, and membership associations.

Since 2004, she has led redesigns for the State of Iowa, New Relic, IBM, Green America, the GIST Network for the U.S. Department of State, and Changemakers for Ashoka: Innovators for the Public, and more. A Drupal user (monicadear) since 2008, she was elected to serve as board member to the global Drupal Association in 2022-2024.

In Michigan's Capital City, she supports LEAP, the Lansing Economic Area Partnership to increase small business revenue and job creation in Ingham, Eaton, and Clinton counties. She reports on $3mm disbursed to "Main Street" businesses as part of the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021.

A recipient of the 2021 FutureHack Hack for Good award and the 2019 and 2018 NTENny Award, Flores was a Pantheon Hero. See her in videos at: http://bit.ly/monicasfloresvideos. She currently speaks at conferences and panels on technology, management, and entrepreneurship.
