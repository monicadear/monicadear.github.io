Monica "Nikki" Flores is a Certified ScrumMasterÂ® and Certified CPACC technical project manager at Lullabot, where she's managed a portfolio of $9mm in award funds to develop enterprise Drupal platforms.

Since 2004, she has led redesigns for the State of Iowa, New Relic, the IBM Selector Tool, Green America, the GIST Network for the U.S. Department of State, and Changemakers for Ashoka: Innovators for the Public, and more. A Drupal (user: monicadear) since 2008, she was elected to serve as board member to the Drupal Association in 2022-2024.

In Michigan's Capital City, she works with the Lansing Economic Area Partnership to increase small business support in Ingham, Eaton, and Clinton counties.

A recipient of the 2018 and 2019 NTENny Award and the 2021 FutureHack Hack for Good, Flores was a Pantheon Hero. She currently speaks at conferences and panels on technology, management, and entrepreneurship.
